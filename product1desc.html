G-Ortus NetGuardian ProX Network Policy Manager

Tailored Solution for Uno Minda

Network Policy Manager

G-Ortus NetGuardian ProX Network Policy Manager stands at the forefront of innovative network access and security solutions, specifically designed to meet the dynamic needs of modern enterprises like Uno Minda.
NetGuardian ProX offers unparalleled flexibility, security, and operational efficiency, making it the ideal choice for securing Uno Minda's corporate LAN/WiFi environments.
G-Ortus NgNPM software is meticulously designed to cater to specific needs and demands, ensuring that it operates with utmost efficiency while being lightweight and highly scalable.

Key Features

Device Administration

Utilizes the RADIUS security protocol to manage network devices, allowing precise control over access and settings adjustments. Network devices can authenticate and authorize device administrator actions via NgNPM, with accounting messages logged for tracking.

Guest and Secure Wireless

Enables secure network access for visitors, contractors, consultants, and customers through web-based and mobile portals. Access privileges for different guest types can be defined, and sponsors assigned to manage guest accounts.

Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)

Allows employees and guests to securely connect their personal devices to the enterprise network. End users can add their devices via configured pathways and provision predefined authentications.

Asset Visibility

Provides continuous visibility and control over network users and devices across wireless, wired, and VPN connections.

Secure Access

Utilizes various authentication protocols such as 802.1X, RADIUS, and MAB to ensure secure network access for devices and endpoints.

Universal Device Compatibility

Supports compatibility with all 802.1X-compliant devices, including PCs (Windows or Linux), Macs, smartphones, and tablets. Additionally, facilitates MAC-based authentication for IoT devices and other non-802.1X capable devices.
